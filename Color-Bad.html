Colors - Improper Uses
[ Worst Sites You've Every Seen! ]


Dokimos.org




There are no words to describe this site. Not only does it have a rainbow background but it is animated (cue shuddering). Enough said really. Do look at this article if you have had a bad day and would like your eyes blown out, but would like a laugh whilst that happens.


Bw-Hilchenbach.de




It’s images are quite randomly placed. I can see that they’ve tried to take the colour scheme of red and blue from the images on the site, and juxtaposing it with yellow to make the primary colours. However it doesn’t really tie together well and the colours are quite displaced.


Gitelink.com




It uses the complementary colours of purple and yellow. Although they are complementary colours, and the colours provide a visual harmony together, overuse of them can result in a garish design. The site also uses different tones of purple and yellow inconsistently.

The dark grey (and white) background does not aid in this site’s aesthetics. It makes the fully saturated purple even more intense, which makes it hard to look at. This is the same with it’s ever-changing font colours on different backgrounds.
